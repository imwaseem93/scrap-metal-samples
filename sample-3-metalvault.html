FAQ

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about selling your scrap to MetalVault.

We accept shipments starting at 500 lbs for specialty alloys like nickel and molybdenum. For tool steels and stainless, our minimum is typically 1,000 lbs. Smaller quantities may be accepted depending on material gradeâ€”contact us for specifics.
Our pricing is based on real-time London Metal Exchange (LME) rates, adjusted for alloy grade and purity. We use certified XRF analyzers to accurately identify material composition on-site, ensuring you get paid for exactly what you have.
MetalVault operates nationwide across all 50 US states, with primary hubs in Texas, Ohio, California, and New Jersey. For larger shipments, we also service Canada and Mexico. Our logistics team coordinates the most efficient pickup routes.
Once material is weighed and verified at our facility, payment is processed immediately. Wire transfers typically arrive same-day or next business day. We also offer check payment and ACH for established partners with net terms available.
Pre-sorting generally results in better pricing since we can pay premium rates for identified grades. However, we accept mixed loads and use XRF testing to separate and grade materials. Our team can advise on the most cost-effective approach for your inventory.
